Our city. Your hair.
Creating from the roots out.

Since opening in 2013 we have been determined to create a relaxing stylish experience without compromising on our planet-friendly beliefs. We know you deserve the best which is why every one of our team members is fully dedicated to continuing education, pushing themselves to be the best at their craft. This means always working hard to provide you with cutting-edge techniques for your coiffure.

We offer complimentary consultations to help you determine the right products and services for your own personal fashion style. We love our clients and always want you to feel confident and happy with a look that will work for you and your lifestyle.

We know all the little things add up which is why we do our best to make you feel welcome and at home every time you visit our salon. Once you’re here our refurbished stations surrounded by greenery and smiling faces will put you at ease. You can feel completely confident knowing that we value you and that your needs are our top priority.

When you leave we want you to not only love your hair, which is very important but to feel relaxed, happy, and ready to take on the world.

We believe that every effort we make, to give back, to improve, to spread kindness, no matter how small is important. We value your input and feedback so never hesitate to ask a question, share a concern, or write a review. We’re here for you!
